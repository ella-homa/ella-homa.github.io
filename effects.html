Turtles

As warmer temperatures are increasing, this results in a smaller ratio of male hatchlings. In addition, it also increases the intensity of the storms, increasing the chances of doing critical damage to the nests.

Tigers

Sea level affects their habitat, making their habitat smaller and smaller- Sundarbans is one of the world’s largest mangrove forests shared by in India and Bangladesh. Mangrove is a type of tree or shrub that grows in mainly tropical coastal swamps that has numerous tangled roots. With the issue of sea levels that are caused by climate change, the forests are getting smaller and smaller, making the tiger’s habitat unbearable.

Polar Bears

Hunger and Habitat Issues- As years and years go by, Polar Bears are at a higher risk as we advance, due to climate change. One of the reasons being with sea level. With the constant issue with sea level, polar bears must wait for the ice to refreeze in order to continue hunting for their prey. In addition, they also depend on the ice platform to rest and breed.

Ocean Acidity

When fossil fuels are burned, they release carbon dioxide waste. The ocean absorbs 30% of this, so the acidity increases, making it less inhabitable for marine life.
