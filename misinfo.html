Common Misinformation About Climate Change

Climate Change is Natural

Climate change is NOT natural. Many people cite the "Ice Age" as an example of the Earth's constant changing climate, but there is a BIG difference between what is going on now and the "Ice Age". The difference is that the "Ice Age" occured naturally without any interference from humans while the Climate Change we are experiencing now is 90% if not entirely the fault of humans and the pollution we have caused.

Climate Change is a Myth

Climate Change is not a myth. Many studies have been conducted and scientists have determind that the Earth is heating up rapidly and if we don't do something soon the damages caused may be irreversible.
