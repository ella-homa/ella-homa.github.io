Water

Use Less Water

  • The less water that needs to be treated reduces the amount of energy and manpower that would've been used otherwise. Small ways you can lower your water usage are, taking shorter showers, turning off the tap when you're brushing your teeth, and not letting the tap run the entire time you're doing the dishes

Use Less Hot Water

  • The energy used to heat up hot water can easily be conserved if you used less water. Simple ways to reduce your hot water usage are washing your laundry in cold water, taking shorter showers, or taking baths instead

Transportation

Walk More

  • Walking and or biking to where you need to go saves energy, fuel, and allows you to get exercise while reducing pollution

Use Public Transportation

  • Public transport saves around 40% of fuel that would be burned if everyone who rode the bus drove their own car

Energy

Use Renewable Energy

  • Using renewable energy helps cut down pollution from fossil fuels.There are many different options such as solar energy, hydroelectricity, and wind energy. Depending on where you live there should be atleast one renewable energy oppurtunity that suits your needs.

Conserve Energy

  • Conserving energy can be as simple as turning off the lights or unplugging the tv when its not in use. When energy is being conservered there is less of a need to burn fuel fpor more energy

Reduce Reuse Recycle

Reduce

    reducing the amount of plastic or non-recyclable products leads to less of those products becoming and aiding in pollution

Reuse

  • Buying a reuseable water bottle or just using the same water bottle stops waste from ending up in landfills, especially if it is plastic which will take over 100 years to decompose

Recycle

  • Recycling allows for less plastic to be made and for less plastic waste to end up in landfills or the ocean.
